Basic Telugu Alphabets (తెలుగు వర్ణమాల)

Telugu alphabets are called Varnamala. They are divided into two main parts:

1. Vowels (అచ్చులు - Achchulu)

These are the basic sounds and can be pronounced independently.

  • అ (a)
  • ఆ (aa)
  • ఇ (i)
  • ఈ (ee)
  • ఉ (u)
  • ఊ (oo)
  • ఎ (e)
  • ఏ (ae)
  • ఐ (ai)
  • ఒ (o)
  • ఓ (oa)
  • ఔ (au)

2. Consonants (హల్లులు - Hallulu)

These need vowels to be pronounced properly.

  • క (ka)
  • ఖ (kha)
  • గ (ga)
  • ఘ (gha)
  • చ (cha)
  • జ (ja)
  • ట (ta)
  • డ (da)
  • త (tha)
  • ద (dha)
  • ప (pa)
  • బ (ba)
  • మ (ma)
  • య (ya)
  • ర (ra)
  • ల (la)
  • వ (va)
  • శ (sha)
  • స (sa)
  • హ (ha)

💡 Tip: Start by practicing vowels first, then combine them with consonants to form words.
