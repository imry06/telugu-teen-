About Telugu Language

Telugu is one of the most widely spoken languages in India. It belongs to the Dravidian language family, which also includes Tamil, Kannada, and Malayalam.

Where is Telugu Spoken?

Telugu is mainly spoken in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. It is also used by Telugu communities across India and around the world.

Number of Speakers

Telugu is spoken by around 80–90 million people worldwide, making it one of the most spoken languages globally.

Telugu Script

Telugu uses its own script which is rounded in shape and written from left to right.

Example:
English: Hello
Telugu: నమస్తే (Namaste)

Nature of Telugu

Telugu is a phonetic language, which means words are pronounced as they are written. This makes learning pronunciation easier.

Basic Grammar Structure

Telugu follows Subject + Object + Verb (SOV) structure.

Hindi: मैं खाना खाता हूँ
Telugu: నేను అన్నం తింటాను (Nenu annam tintanu)

Fun Fact

Telugu is often called the "Italian of the East" because of its musical and vowel-rich sound.
