Basic Pronunciation (ఉచ్చారణ)

Telugu pronunciation is very phonetic. This means words are pronounced exactly as they are written.

1. Vowel Sounds

  • అ → a (like "about")
  • ఆ → aa (like "father")
  • ఇ → i (like "bit")
  • ఈ → ee (like "see")
  • ఉ → u (like "put")
  • ఊ → oo (like "food")
  • ఎ → e (like "pen")
  • ఏ → ae (like "say")
  • ఐ → ai (like "ice")
  • ఒ → o (like "pot")
  • ఓ → oa (like "go")
  • ఔ → au (like "cow")

2. Consonant Sounds

Consonants are pronounced with an inherent "a" sound unless combined with other vowels.

  • క → ka
  • గ → ga
  • చ → cha
  • జ → ja
  • ట → ta (hard)
  • త → tha (soft)
  • ద → dha
  • ప → pa
  • బ → ba
  • మ → ma

3. Combining Sounds

  • క + ఆ = కా (kaa)
  • గ + ఇ = గి (gi)
  • త + ఉ = తు (thu)
  • ప + ఓ = పో (po)

💡 Tip: Break words into small parts (syllables) like "నేను" → ne-nu to improve pronunciation.
